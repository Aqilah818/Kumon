Our Programmes

English

Daily Kumon English Worksheets are designed to help your child develop strong reading and comprehension skills by working at their own pace. Each worksheet focuses on building a solid foundation, allowing your child to improve their critical reading skills before progressing to more advanced materials. This step-by-step approach fosters independent learning and a deeper interest in reading. By practicing daily, your child develops a consistent routine, enhances their ability to analyze texts, and gradually acquires the skills needed to approach advanced materials with independence and confidence.

Mathematics

Daily Kumon Math Worksheets are designed to help your child develop strong math skills by working at their own pace in a structured and supportive way. Each worksheet focuses on building a solid foundation, allowing your child to master one concept thoroughly before progressing to the next. This step-by-step approach not only boosts confidence but also fosters independent learning and critical thinking skills. By practicing daily, your child develops a consistent routine, sharpens their focus, and gradually improves their problem-solving abilities.
